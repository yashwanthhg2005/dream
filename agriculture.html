Agriculture Land

Fertile Grounds, Modern Farming, and Contract Leasing Opportunities

Farmland Overview

We manage a total 20 acres of highly fertile agricultural land situated near major water bodies. Our lands are cultivated using sustainable, modern farming techniques to maximize yield without degrading the soil quality. We offer commercial cropping, contract farming, and land leasing opportunities to farmers and agritech companies.

Land Size Details

Total Area: 20 Acres
Crop Split: 8 Acres Sugarcane, 6 Acres Paddy, 8 Acres Coconut Trees

Soil Variety

Type: Red loamy to moderate clay
pH Level: Balanced 6.5 - 7.5 (Crop versatile)

Irrigation robust Facilities

  • Uninterrupted canal water supply (Season based)
  • Multiple deep-bore wells providing year-round groundwater
  • Drip irrigation and sprinkler systems pre-installed in specific blocks
  • Dedicated 3-phase agricultural electricity connections

Primary Crops Cultivated

  • Cash Crops: Sugarcane, Cotton
  • Grains: Paddy (Sona Masoori, RNR), Ragi, Maize
  • Horticulture: Coconut groves, Mango Orchards
