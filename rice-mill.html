Sri Lakshmi Devi Rice Mill

Premium Quality Grains processed with Advanced Technology at Basarallu

Business Overview

Sri Lakshmi Devi Rice Mill is dedicated to producing the finest quality rice through modern milling technology. With decades of experience in grain processing, we ensure that every grain meets the highest standards of hygiene, taste, and nutrition.

Services Offered

  • Paddy processing to refined raw/boiled rice
  • Rice polishing and sortex cleaning
  • Wholesale supply and bulk packaging
  • Custom milling services for farmers

Advantages of Our Rice

  • 100% Sortex Clean: Free from stones, husk, and broken grains
  • Aged to Perfection: We ensure perfect aging for the best fluffiness when cooked
  • Nutrient Retention: Advanced milling ensures vital nutrients are preserved
  • Unadulterated: Pure, chemical-free processing guarantees natural taste

Machinery Details

Our facility is equipped with fully automated Japanese machinery including De-stoners, Paddy Separators, Multi-pass Polishers, and high-resolution Optical Color Sorters capable of processing 10 Tons per hour.
