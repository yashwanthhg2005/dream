Sri Lakshmi Devi Saw Mill

Premium Timber Processing and Custom Wood Cutting at Hallegere

Business Description

Sri Lakshmi Devi Saw Mill offers comprehensive wood processing solutions for construction, furniture making, and architectural design. We source sustainable, high-quality timber and employ skilled craftsmen utilizing precision machinery to deliver impeccably finished wood products to our diverse clientele.

Types of Wood Cutting Services

  • Band saw cutting for large logs into planks
  • Precision circular saw cutting for custom dimensions
  • Planing and smoothing services
  • Wood turning and shaping
  • Treatments (seasoning, anti-termite)

Advantages of Our Mill

  • Accuracy: Millimeter-perfect precision with digital measurement systems
  • Wide Inventory: Teak, Sal, Rosewood, Pine, and Rubberwood available
  • Minimal Wastage: Optimized cutting plans to ensure maximum yield
  • Timely Delivery: Scale of operations allows fast turnaround for bulk orders
